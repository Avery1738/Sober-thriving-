🚨 Urge Toolkit

Go here the moment a craving hits. Before anything else.

Urges peak at 20–30 minutes. Every single time. Then they fade.
Your only job is the next 10 minutes.
1
Stop
Put everything down. Don't scroll. Don't text. Just stop. 60 seconds.
2
Name it out loud
"I am having a craving right now. That is all it is. It will pass."
3
Locate it in your body
Where do you feel it? Chest? Stomach? Just notice. Don't fight.
4
Breathe — 4-4-6
In for 4 counts. Hold 4. Out for 6. Tap the button below to guide you.
5
One action from your Coping Menu
Not everything. Just one. Go to coping menu →
6
Call someone right now
If the wave is too strong — call your person. Before anything else.
